Link Agro – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 18 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Link Agro (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the Link Agro mobile application (“App”) and how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide while using our App. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information that you directly provide inside the App.

1.1 Account Registration and Login

To create and access your account, you provide:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Password

These details are used only for authentication and account management.

1.2 Login

  • Mobile number
  • Password

1.3 Delivery Details

You provide:

  • Delivery address
  • Mobile number

This information is used for delivering your order and contacting you when required.

1.4 Location (Only When You Choose to Share)

Your location is collected only when you tap “Share My Location” in the order details page.

We collect:

  • Latitude
  • Longitude

This location is used only to update delivery details for the specific order.

1.5 Local Storage (Stored on Your Device)

Using Shared Preferences, the App stores:

  • User ID
  • User name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Profile image URL
  • Pincode
  • Login type
  • Login status
  • First-launch status
  • Language & theme preferences
  • Cart items
  • FCM notification token

This data stays on your device and is removed when you log out or uninstall the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Register and authenticate your account
  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Update delivery address and location
  • Display order history and details
  • Maintain your login session
  • Send order and notification updates
  • Improve overall app experience

We do not use your information for advertising.

3. Sharing of Information

We share your information only when necessary to provide the service.

3.1 API Server

Your details are shared securely with our server for:

  • Registering an account
  • Logging in
  • Updating address
  • Updating location
  • Managing orders

3.2 Delivery Partner

To complete your order, we share:

  • Name
  • Delivery address
  • Mobile number
  • Shared location (if you choose to provide it)

3.3 Firebase Services

Used only for:

  • Push notifications

No passwords or sensitive information are stored in Firebase.

4. Data Security

We follow industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted communication
  • Secure password handling
  • Protected local storage

We take all reasonable steps to protect your information.

5. Data Retention

  • Account details remain until you request deletion
  • Delivery location is used only for the order you share it for
  • Local storage data stays on your device until you log out or uninstall
  • OTP is temporary and not saved

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Update your profile information
  • Edit your delivery details
  • Choose whether to share your location
  • Log out or clear app data
  • Request account deletion

For deletion or support, contact us: info@linkagrofresh.com

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy anytime. The latest version will always be available in the App or on the Play Store.

9. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy:
